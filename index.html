System Defense

System Defense is a tower-defense game where you have to keep the base from getting destroyed by multiple hordes of enemies.


Protect the base! Place down turrets that will attack the enemies and keep the base from getting destroyed.


Controls: W, A, S, D to look around.

Zooming In And Out: Scrollwheel

Placing a turret: Click on a turret icon (in the bottom menu) to select it and then click on a white empty space to place it.

Upgrading/Selling a turret: Click on a turret placed on a white space and it will show you two options above the turret: One to upgrade and one to sell.

Kill Cube

Kill Cube is a short and simple game I made for a game jam called Wowie Jam 3.0 which lasted for 3 days. (February 26th 2021 at 10:00 PM to March 1st 2021 at 10:10 PM)

A game jam is an event where participants need to make a game based of a theme within a limited amount of time. After some time, the games submitted by people can then be played by other people for them to vote. The game with the best feedback and votes will be the winner.

The square (in the middle) about to die to the spikes in order to proceed.
In the game. There will be obstacles blocking your character (the square), in order to proceed, you have to kill the character to make the obstacles move out of the way.

The character reaching the end (the red flag on the right)
In the game. There will be obstacles blocking your character (the square), in order to proceed, you have to kill the character to make the obstacles move out of the way.

I enjoyed doing this game jam as I think it gave me a lot of exposure onto what creating a game will feel like (having to do the code, music, sounds and everything else by myself) This was also my first time trying to make music and I think there could be some room for improvement, but other than that, I am quite happy with the rest of the game.
